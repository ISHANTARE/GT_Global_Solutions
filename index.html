Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our services

What products do you export?
We export a diverse range of products including agricultural goods (rice, spices, tea, coffee), textiles & garments, industrial machinery, handicrafts & home decor, food products, and more. Our product portfolio is constantly expanding to meet global demand.
Which countries do you export to?
We currently export to 50+ countries across North America, Europe, Middle East, Africa, Southeast Asia, and Australia. Our key markets include the USA, UK, Germany, UAE, Japan, and Australia. We are continuously expanding our global footprint.
What are your payment terms?
We offer flexible payment terms including Advance Payment (T/T), Letter of Credit (L/C), Documentary Collection (D/P, D/A), and PayPal for smaller orders. Payment terms are negotiable based on order volume and business relationship history.
Do you offer CIF/FOB/EXW shipping?
Yes, we offer all major Incoterms including FOB (Free on Board), CIF (Cost, Insurance & Freight), EXW (Ex Works), CFR, and DDP. The shipping terms can be customized based on your requirements and destination.
How can I request a quote?
You can request a quote through our Contact Us page, by emailing info@globaltradeexports.com, or by calling us directly. Please include product details, quantity requirements, destination, and preferred Incoterms for a faster response.
What certifications do you have?
We hold an Import Export Code (IEC) from DGFT, GST Registration, MCCI Membership (Merchants Chamber of Commerce & Industry), and additional trade body certifications. Our products comply with international quality and safety standards.
Do you provide import services as well?
Absolutely! We offer comprehensive import services including sourcing raw materials, machinery, electronics, and consumer goods from international markets. Our global procurement network ensures competitive pricing and quality assurance.
What is the minimum order quantity?
Minimum order quantities vary by product category. Generally, we accommodate orders from 1 metric ton for agricultural products and from 500 pieces for textiles/handicrafts. Contact us for product-specific MOQ details and we'll be happy to assist.
How do I track my shipment?
Once your order is shipped, we provide a tracking number and carrier details via email. You can track your shipment in real-time through the carrier's website. Our logistics team also provides regular updates until delivery is confirmed.
Do you offer domestic supply services?
Yes, we offer comprehensive domestic trade and supply chain services across India. This includes sourcing, warehousing, inventory management, and last-mile delivery for businesses of all sizes. Contact us for customized domestic supply solutions.
