Bulk Export Supply

GT Global Solutions provides comprehensive export solutions designed to help Indian manufacturers and suppliers reach global markets efficiently. Our end-to-end approach covers bulk export supply with competitive pricing.

  • Product Sourcing & Procurement - We connect with verified manufacturers across India to source quality products at competitive prices.
  • Quality Inspection & Testing - Every shipment undergoes rigorous quality checks before dispatch to ensure compliance with international standards.
  • Documentation & Compliance - Complete export documentation including commercial invoices, packing lists, certificates of origin, and customs clearance.
  • Logistics & Shipping - Sea freight, air freight, and multimodal transport solutions with real-time tracking and reliable delivery timelines.
  • Market Research & Advisory - Insights on target markets, trade regulations, tariffs, and pricing strategies to maximize your export potential.

Global Sourcing & Import

Providing global sourcing supplies to Indian customers. We ensure all documentation & compliance transparency. If you need any imported items, we can source and deliver them for you.

Our import team works with verified international suppliers to procure the exact products you need, handling everything from initial sourcing to final delivery.

  • Global Sourcing Network - Access to thousands of verified suppliers across 30+ countries.
  • Competitive Pricing - We negotiate the best rates leveraging our long-standing supplier relationships.
  • Customs Clearance - Complete import documentation and customs processing for hassle-free delivery.
  • Quality Assurance - Pre-shipment inspections at source to ensure product quality meets your specifications.

OEM & Custom Branding

Beyond standard export and import, GT Global Solutions offers comprehensive OEM and private labeling services. We connect with trusted manufacturers across India's manufacturing hubs to create your custom products.

  • Custom Configurations - Tailored product manufacturing as per customer requirements worldwide.
  • Private Labeling - Custom branding and bespoke packaging solutions.
  • Reliable Network - Deep connections with trusted manufacturers and artisan supply chains.
  • Quality Standards - Rigorous quality control and pre-shipment inspection processes.
